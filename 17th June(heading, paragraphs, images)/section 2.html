Coolen Hoover

Early Life→

Colleen Hoover was born on December 11, 1979, in Sulphur Springs, Texas. She spent her childhood in the small town of Saltillo, where she developed a love for reading and storytelling. Despite facing financial struggles growing up, she remained passionate about books and creativity. She later pursued a degree in social work and worked in various roles, including teaching and counseling, before discovering her talent for writing fiction.

Her novels are known for their emotional depth, realistic characters, and themes that explore relationships, healing, and personal growth.

Achievements →

Colleen Hoover has become one of the most successful contemporary authors in the world. Many of her books have topped the New York Times bestseller list and have been widely discussed in reading communities like BookTok. Her novel It Ends With Us is especially praised for its sensitive portrayal of domestic violence and empowerment. Her success as a self-published author who rose to global fame is seen as an inspiration by many aspiring writers.
