It Ends With Us

It Ends With Us tells the powerful story of Lily Bloom, who faces a difficult decision between love and survival. Through her relationship with Ryle Kincaid and memories of Atlas, her first love, Lily discovers what it truly means to end the cycle of abuse and start anew.

It Starts With Us

It Starts With Us is the sequel to It Ends With Us and continues the journey of Lily Bloom as she attempts to move forward with her life after an abusive relationship. The novel highlights healing, forgiveness, and a second chance at love with Atlas Corrigan, while also dealing with the challenges of co-parenting.

Verity

Verity is a psychological thriller centered on struggling writer Lowen Ashleigh, who is hired to finish bestselling author Verity Crawford’s book series. In the Crawford home, Lowen uncovers a hidden manuscript that blurs the line between fiction and reality, revealing horrifying secrets about Verity and her family.
